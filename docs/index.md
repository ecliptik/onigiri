# Onigiri 🍙

A personal calorie, nutrition, and water tracker for iPhone, iPad, and
Apple Watch. Everything logged is saved to Apple Health — there's no
account to sign up for, and your data always remains yours.

## What it does

- **Calorie & nutrition tracking** — what you ate minus what you burned
  (Apple Health active + resting energy), with the calories left for
  the day.
- **Weight goal** — set a target weight and date; Onigiri computes the
  daily calorie budget and projects your finish date from your real
  weight trend.
- **Barcode & label scanning** — scan a barcode or photograph the
  nutrition-facts label and the numbers fill themselves in.
- **Fast logging** — a tap or two per entry, everything editable, one-tap
  undo.
- **Custom meals** — bundle foods you eat together, log the whole thing
  at once.
- **Water tracking** — from the app, a widget, the watch, or a long-press
  on the add button.
- **Apple Watch app** — log on the go, check your balance at a glance,
  complications for your watch face.
- **Widgets** — home-screen and lock-screen widgets, plus a Control
  Center water button.
- **Private by design** — logs live in Apple Health on your device; the
  app has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.
